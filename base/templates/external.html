We are part of the BSC.

We are a member house of the Berkeley Student Cooperative, an organization founded to provide low-cost, high quality housing to under-privileged students of UC Berkeley and other Bay Area colleges and universities.

To apply to live at Afro, visit the BSC's application page.

To apply to board at Afro, visit the BSC's boarding information page.

Or drop by for dinner. We are accepting house-level boarders.

We are located at 2347 Prospect Street, Berkeley, CA 94704 (click for map).


We are partners with our community.

We host events for African-American student and community groups.

We actively do community service in reaching out to and helping the cause of African-American groups in the greater Bay Area.

In our boarding and housing candidate selection, we give priority preference to members of African-American student organizations.

If your values align with ours, we encourage you to reach out to us. E-mail our house manager at athhm@bsc.coop.
